Sajal Maheshwari

I am an engineer at Qualcomm, where I work with the autonomous driving team.

At Qualcomm I am currently working in the Sensor fusion team, trying to generate a robust autonomous driving system using multiple sensors and a combination of classical and deep learning based methods. Previously, I have worked with the Camera team in Qualcomm India, and developed algorithms for better dynamic range and exposure. I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University, with a Masters degree in computer vision in December 2020 where I worked with Dr. Michael Kaess. Before that, I completed my B.Tech. with Honours from International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad, in 2017, where I worked with Dr. Vineet Gandhi.
